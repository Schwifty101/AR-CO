- Registration valid for 10 years

### Process Flow

**STEP 1: Search & Assessment (2-3 days)**
- Trademark search in IPO database
- Check for similar marks
- Assess registrability

**STEP 2: Application Preparation (3-5 days)**

- Classification of goods/services
- Logo preparation (if applicable)

**STEP 3: Filing (1 day)**
- File with IPO Pakistan
- Pay government fees
- Receive filing receipt

**STEP 4: Examination (3-4 months)**
- IPO examines application
- Handle objections (if any)
- Respond to queries

**STEP 5: Publication (1 month)**
- Published in Trade Marks Journal
- Opposition period (2 months)

**STEP 6: Registration (1-2 months)**
